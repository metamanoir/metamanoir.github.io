title: 'Velvet Statue & Its Doll'
description: 'type shii'
pubDate: 'May 23 2026'

"Hand it to MEE" The velvet statue screamed  
Turning...  
Hundred steps away I hear it  
Its doll resting in my hands  

I count my steps back  
"The BOOK!" it screams once again  
The layers of robe slaps the air  
As it points to the giant paperless book  
Put on the tiny dying desk  
Under the tower's lonely window  
Floating over the dry sea  

I place the doll on the spot  
Turning...  
"LIGHT!" it yells once more  
Flapping its wings of silk  
Posing in the opposite  
Pointing with its one finger    
To where all light sleeps    
I tilt the doll a slight  
And the light wake up  
Fleeing back to their gods
