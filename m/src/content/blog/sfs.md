"Seventy Five Seven" Written alphabetically on the concrete floor  
To your opposite, a breathing refrigator rests  
Your gaze not daring to look away  
Bones stiff from sitting  
In humilation they beg for sharpening  
Yet your muscles disobey

A sudden faint presence flashes  
On your four new legs    
No longer a human
You hasten to see whom  
No one....  
None...  
Nada...  
And you'll regret it

Why?  
Y--  
And no soul was there  
Only the refrigator  
Unplugged, functional it remains  
Buzzing and flickering  
You squat to scan its organs

*"It's Pregnante"* 

You remark  
With a cracked mirror  
And through it  
You see what can only be heard  
Hear what can only be tasted  
Taste what can only be  

Divine bullshit frames fries your neurons  
And a grafted creature caresses you  
Whispers to your hand  

*"Five were that... And seven were this..."*  
*"Prrrrrrrrrrrrr... Etus, Sel"*  
*"Maybe it wasn't that bad after all"*  

Trapped and in no control  
A worm head eats your hand and seals it  
From the creature's many tails it emerged  
*"O How beautiful!"*  
*"Prrrrrrrrrrrrr... Etus, Sel"*  
The creature claps  
A new set of chains  
It stitches  
You wear  
Forced to dance with the madness  
At least you're both content  
And only you can suffer the consequences
